Privacy Policy

ZigZag - Instagram Reels Downloader & Video Management Tool

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

ZigZag is a video management tool that processes Instagram content. We collect:

  • Instagram URLs - Public Instagram reel URLs you provide for downloading
  • Video Content - Downloaded video files from public Instagram posts
  • Generated Descriptions - AI-generated descriptions of your video content
  • Usage Data - Basic usage statistics to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Download and process Instagram reels from public URLs you provide
  • Generate AI-powered descriptions for your video content
  • Organize and rename your video files
  • Improve our service functionality and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API - For generating AI-powered video descriptions
  • Instagram/Meta APIs - For accessing public Instagram content
  • Instaloader Library - For downloading Instagram content

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Store your data on our servers
  • Share your personal information with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data during processing.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete downloaded videos and generated content
  • Stop using our service at any time
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

6. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You control how long your data is retained
  • Deleting files from your device removes them permanently
  • We do not maintain copies of your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
